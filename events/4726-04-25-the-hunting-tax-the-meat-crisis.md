What Happened: About two weeks in, the grain supplies dwindled. The werefolk, being natural hunters with wilderness survival skills, took over hunting in the surrounding woods. However, they kept the choicest cuts of meat for themselves, bringing back only scraps, bones, and questionable "mystery meat" for Mother Spoons to throw into the communal slumfolk stew. The Consequence: The werefolk have grown arrogant, treating the slumfolk not as equals, but as a weak, dependent "herd." In retaliation, the slumfolk—using skills from the streets of Stormhaven—started pickpocketing the werefolk and stealing the good meat while they slept. The Decision: Resource Law and Taxation. The players must decide who "owns" the resources of the land.

Do the werefolk have a right to keep what they kill?
Is there a flat "tax" of meat that goes to the fort's communal stores?
Cato (Asmodean) and Casseus will likely want rigid rationing, but doing so means threatening heavily armed, hungry werewolves.
