Upon returning to port after being intercepted by [The Free Sails](../factions/free-sails.md) the party was met by [Captain Zephyr Swiftwind](../npcs/zephyr-swiftwind.md).

He told them that he had conviced the other captains not to retaliate without first talking to the party.
He offers to have some men stationed at their fort, which would contribute to the fort.

They would be smugglers, using the fort as a place to do business and a base of sorts.

The cost of saying no was made clear to be a far less nice offer, and they won't be allowed to ship their materials to the fort, or be allowed to keep the fort secret.
