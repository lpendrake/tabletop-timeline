---
title: The "Pack-Thrall" Protection Racket
date: 4726-04-28
---
What Happened: Without the heavily armored adventurers around, the terrifying reality of living in a ruined fort in the wilderness set in. The werefolk, bolstered by their Erastil clerics who actually know how to survive and build community in the wild, became the undeniable top dogs. Seeing the raw power of the werefolk, a sub-group of the slumfolk voluntarily submitted to them, acting as servants, groomers, and cooks (essentially settling into "bitch" roles) in exchange for physical protection and guaranteed meat.

The Consequence: The slumfolk are now deeply fractured. The two newly anointed Iroran acolytes are fundamentally disgusted by this; they preach that submitting to beasts out of fear is a complete abandonment of their physical and mental potential. Meanwhile, the Pharasman/Iroran medics are stuck patching up people on both sides of this social divide. The "thrall" slumfolk are adopting wolf-like mannerisms, and the werefolk are aggressively protective of "their" humans, treating them like prized pets. The Decision: Social Equality vs. Natural Hierarchy.

Do the players outlaw this voluntary servitude?
Because Cato is heavily disliked and Casseus is feared, the "thralls" might actually view the werefolk as better masters than the party.
Frollo is going to have to decide how to respond to his two new Iroran acolytes, who are likely demanding the party break up these "packs" to restore human dignity, even if the thralls don't want to leave.
