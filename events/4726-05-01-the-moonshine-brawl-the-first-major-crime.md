What Happened: A week ago, stress boiled over. Some slumfolk had secretly used some of Mother Spoons' rationed grain to brew harsh moonshine. They sold it to the werefolk. Drunk and rowdy, a young werewolf shifted and badly mauled one of the slumfolk brewers. During the chaos, the werefolk's personal belongings were looted by opportunistic slumfolk. The Consequence: The slumfolk have barricaded themselves in the central keep, refusing to do any structural repairs while the werewolves roam free. The werewolves have taken over the courtyard. Mother Spoons has one werewolf locked in an old cellar cell, but the werefolk are threatening to break him out if the slumfolk don't return their stolen goods. The Decision: Justice & Punishment. The party must try the first major crimes of their new society.

How do you punish a violent mauling from a were-creature?
How do you punish the illicit brewing/theft from the slumfolk?
Cato (playing the Hellknight) will likely want strict, brutal punishment (floggings or chains), while Frollo (worshipper of Irori) might advocate for restorative physical labor. Setting the legal precedent here will define the fort's laws forever.
