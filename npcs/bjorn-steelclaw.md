# Bjorn Steelclaw

Ancestry: Human
Proffession: Pack Leader, ex-smith

Leader of a group of werewolf renegades who had been on the run for nearly a decade. Convinced by the party to take his people through the woods to [Newhaven](../locations/newhaven.md) rather than risk staying near [Stormhaven](../locations/stormhaven.md).

Now third in command at the fortress, behind [Elara "Mother Spoons" Coppertress](elara-coppertress.md), who is the elected leader while the party is away.
He's stern, used to ruling 50% by force, 50% by force of personality, in a world that wants him and his people dead for being cursed.
