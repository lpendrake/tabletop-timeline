# Vitoria

**Ancestry:** Elf
**Class:** Companion Inventor
**Weapon:** Short bow
**Worships:** [Pharasma](../factions/pharasma.md)
**Focus:** Her companion Gary the robot (class primary feature), team healer via Medic Dedication and Ancient Elf giving Cleric of Pharasma Dedication. Wildly effective healing so long as there aren't too many fights per day. The Pharasman cleric angle has been a major campaign driver.

### Key facts
- Charged by [Lady So-Yun](../npcs/so-yun-nightshade.md) to keep an eye on the team and prevent them causing too much political trouble in town. That's not going great.
- The team took over a nearby fortress (2–4 days march away), staffed it with poor people and criminal werewolf renegades on the run for nearly a decade.
- They're secretly funnelling building materials to the fort and have acquired a ship from the Abadarian inquisitor — formerly a [Free Sails](../factions/free-sails.md) mobile drug lab operated by an alchemist from the House of Storms.
- This has pissed off the Free Sails, the House of Storms, and the Gozrans.
- They've also taken part in fracking in the hills around Stormhaven on behalf of the [Smokers](../factions/the-smokers.md) and Carvers, which pissed off the druids.
